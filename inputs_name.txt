Use these strings for Input.GetAxis() 

E.G : 

if(Input.GetAxis("P1_X") > 0){
	// Player 1 jumps
}


List for Player 1. For other players, replace P1 by P2, P3 or P4

P1_Horizontal
P1_Vertical
P1_Square
P1_X
P1_Circle
P1_Triangle
P1_L1
P1_R1
P1_L2
P1_R2
